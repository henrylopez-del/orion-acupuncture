01 — Approach

Five rhythms inside one body.

In Traditional Chinese Medicine your body holds five rhythms: wood, fire, earth, metal, water. When one runs too loud or too quiet, the others bend around it. Sleep frays, cycles stutter, digestion slows, mood shifts.

Every session begins by listening for which one is leading. The pulse on each wrist, the colour and coat of the tongue, the shape of your week. Then we choose the points that move the rhythm back.

02 — Your first session

Ninety minutes to read the body.

i.

Intake

We talk about sleep, appetite, cycle, mood, how often you sigh. Then we read your pulse on both wrists and look at your tongue. No paperwork shortcuts.

ii.

Treatment

Forty-five minutes on a heated table. Twelve to twenty very fine needles, sometimes moxa, sometimes cupping. You'll feel a few of them. Most patients fall asleep.

iii.

Aftercare

A printed protocol: foods, herbs, simple rituals for the week. We don't recommend supplements you can't pronounce. We do recommend a hot foot soak before bed.
