01· The Approach

Five rhythms inside one body.

In TCM, the body holds five rhythms: wood, fire, earth, metal, water. When one runs too loud or too quiet, the others bend around it. Sleep frays. Cycles stutter. Mood shifts.

Every session starts the same way. We read the pulse on both wrists, look at the tongue, listen to how you describe your week. Most of the diagnosis happens before any needle goes in.

02· What we treat

Specific, not generic.

Stress-driven sleep loss is the largest group at the clinic. Then cycle and fertility support, often alongside IVF protocols. Then digestion, then skin, then post-illness recovery.

Most patients see meaningful change within four sessions. If it isn't moving by six, we change the plan or refer out. The caseload is deliberately narrow so the work stays sharp.

04· Your first session· 90 min

Ninety minutes to read the body.

A long intake first. We talk about sleep, appetite, cycle, mood, how often you sigh. Pulse on both wrists. Tongue. Then forty-five minutes on a heated table, twelve to twenty fine needles, sometimes moxa or cupping.

You leave with a printed protocol for the week. Foods, herbs, simple rituals. Not a list of supplements you can't pronounce. We do recommend a hot foot soak before bed.
